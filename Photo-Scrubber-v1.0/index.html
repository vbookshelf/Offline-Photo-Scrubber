Photo Scrubber GitHub

Select or drag & drop photos

Supports JPEG & PNG
Photo Scrubber removes metadata from your photos.
All photos are processed in the browser, on your device.
Your photos are never sent to an external server.
Disclaimer: Provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. While this tool is designed to strip privacy-compromising EXIF/GPS data, always manually verify mission-critical photos before public distribution. A simple process for manual verification is explained in the GitHub readme.
